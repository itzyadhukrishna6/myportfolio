Donut Experience / UI/UX Case Study

UI/UX Design / Visual Identity

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer, Visual Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Overview

This project explores the intersection of visual storytelling and user experience through a donut themed interactive interface designed to captivate and retain user attention. The core This project explores the fusion of visual storytelling and user experience through a donut themed interactive interface designed to captivate users. The goal was to transform a simple concept into an immersive digital experience by focusing on motion, minimalism, and strong visual hierarchy, addressing the repetitive nature of typical food related designs.

The design process was driven by research into modern UI/UX trends, emphasizing clean layouts enhanced with subtle animations. A central visual element the donut was used as the core focus to guide user attention, supported by a carefully balanced color palette and minimal typography to maintain clarity and elegance

Design Process

Motion design played a key role in creating a smooth and engaging experience, where animations were used functionally to guide interaction rather than as mere decoration. The main challenge was balancing creativity with usability, which was achieved through iterative refinement and a focus on simplicity. The final outcome is a polished, modern interface that highlights the impact of thoughtful design and purposeful animation in enhancing user engagement. This project reflects a user centered approach that prioritizes clarity, emotion, and interaction, with strong potential for future expansion into a fully functional product.
